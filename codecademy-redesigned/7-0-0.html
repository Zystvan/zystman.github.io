Codecademy Redesigned Version 7.0.0

New Features

Redesigned Forums

The way the Forums used to look got to be a pain on the eyes after a while - so bright and not enough contrast. Well, now they use slightly darker colors with more contrast, so you can stay helping others longer with much less eye stress!

Canned Responses

Tired of typing the same thing over and over again in the forums? Now you can save a response and just click a button to insert it into your post, rather than having to retype it over and over again!

Fixed notification bell

The notification bell used to link to /notifications on the main site - but because of the forum page, /notifications was useless. So we made the bell link to your Discuss notifications, enabling you to quickly get to your notifications any time.
